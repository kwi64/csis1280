DISCOVER ENCHANTING TRAILS

Lynn Headwaters Regional Park

Nestled within the majestic North Shore Mountains, Lynn Headwaters Regional Park beckons adventurers with its pristine wilderness and captivating landscapes. Traverse ancient forests adorned with towering trees, venture along the rushing waters of Lynn Creek, and marvel at panoramic vistas of snow-capped peaks. This outdoor playground offers a plethora of activities for nature enthusiasts, from tranquil hikes through fern-filled valleys to exhilarating mountain biking trails. Discover hidden waterfalls, spot wildlife in their natural habitat, and immerse yourself in the serenity of this remarkable park. Whether you seek a peaceful escape or thrilling outdoor pursuits, Lynn Headwaters Regional Park promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of British Columbia's untamed wilderness.

Grouse Mountain Regional Park

Escape to the heights of adventure at Grouse Mountain Regional Park, where breathtaking vistas and thrilling activities await. Ascend by Skyride to the summit, where sweeping views of the city skyline and surrounding wilderness unfold before you. Embark on scenic hiking trails that meander through alpine meadows and dense forests, offering encounters with local wildlife and picturesque viewpoints along the way. In winter, carve through pristine snow on ski slopes or enjoy the enchantment of snowshoeing through a winter wonderland. For adrenaline seekers, test your courage on the mountain's zip lines and aerial obstacle courses. Grouse Mountain is a year-round playground for outdoor enthusiasts, blending natural beauty with exhilarating experiences high above Vancouver.

Joffre Lakes Provincial Park

Discover the awe-inspiring beauty of Joffre Lakes Provincial Park, a pristine alpine wonderland that captivates visitors with its shimmering turquoise lakes and towering mountain peaks. Embark on a scenic hike along well-maintained trails that lead you through lush forests and open meadows, offering breathtaking views at every turn. Marvel at the vivid colors of Lower, Middle, and Upper Joffre Lakes, each more enchanting than the last, reflecting the surrounding glaciers and sky above. Capture memorable photographs against the backdrop of Matier Glacier and Mount Joffre, which dominate the landscape. Whether you're a seasoned hiker seeking adventure or a nature lover in search of tranquility, Joffre Lakes Provincial Park promises an unforgettable escape into the heart of British Columbia's wilderness.

Joffre Lakes Provincial Park

Escape to the wilderness of Seymour Provincial Park, where rugged landscapes and outdoor adventures beckon. Explore a network of trails that wind through dense forests of towering Douglas fir and cedar trees, offering opportunities for hiking, mountain biking, and wildlife viewing. Discover hidden waterfalls, tranquil creeks, and scenic viewpoints that showcase panoramic vistas of the surrounding mountains and valleys. In winter, Seymour transforms into a snowy playground, inviting visitors to ski, snowboard, and snowshoe amidst pristine alpine terrain. Challenge yourself on the park's ski slopes or enjoy a peaceful winter hike under the towering trees. Seymour Provincial Park is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, providing year-round excitement and natural beauty just a short drive from Vancouver.
