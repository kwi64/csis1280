CULINARY DELIGHTS AMIDST NATURAL BEAUTY

Fable Kitchen

Indulge in a delightful culinary experience at Fable Kitchen, a charming restaurant nestled in the heart of Vancouver's Kitsilano neighborhood. Fable Kitchen takes pride in serving locally sourced, seasonal ingredients transformed into delectable dishes that celebrate the flavors of the Pacific Northwest. Led by renowned Chef Trevor Bird, the menu showcases inventive creations that highlight the best of British Columbia's bounty, from fresh seafood to organic produce. Enjoy signature dishes like the famous Fable Burger made with house-ground beef and artisanal cheeses, or savor innovative vegetarian and vegan options crafted with care. Pair your meal with a selection from the thoughtfully curated wine list or creative cocktails. Whether you're seeking a casual brunch, a romantic dinner, or a memorable culinary experience, Fable Kitchen promises exceptional food and hospitality in a welcoming setting.

Delara

Experience the rich flavors of Persian cuisine at Delara, a contemporary restaurant located in the vibrant city of Vancouver. Delara takes pride in its unique blend of traditional and modern Persian dishes, all meticulously crafted from locally sourced ingredients. Under the expert guidance of Chef Bardia Ilbeiggi, who infuses his Persian heritage into each dish, the menu offers a refreshing take on Middle Eastern fare. Delara is more than just a kebab place, it’s a culinary journey that brings farm-to-table dining to a whole new level. Enjoy signature dishes like the succulent lamb kebab or the aromatic saffron rice, and don’t forget to try the innovative vegetarian options. Complement your meal with a selection from their carefully curated wine list or enjoy one of their creative cocktails. Whether you’re looking for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a unique culinary adventure, Delara promises an unforgettable dining experience in a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

Cioppino's

Savor the flavors of the Mediterranean at Cioppino’s, an acclaimed restaurant nestled in the heart of Vancouver’s vibrant Yaletown district. Cioppino’s takes pride in offering a blend of traditional and contemporary Italian and Mediterranean dishes, all crafted with the finest locally sourced ingredients. Guided by the culinary expertise of Executive Chef Pino Posteraro, the menu features a variety of innovative creations that highlight the best of Mediterranean cuisine. From the classic lobster bisque to the house-cured organic pork salami, each dish promises a unique gastronomic experience. Pair your meal with a selection from their extensive wine list or enjoy a handcrafted cocktail. Whether you’re planning a business dinner, a romantic evening, or a special celebration, Cioppino’s promises an unforgettable dining experience in an elegant setting.
